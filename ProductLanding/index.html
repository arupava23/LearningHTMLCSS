About Bipad

Bipad Saha, an exceptional individual, embodies resilience, determination, and a deep commitment to community service. Born in war-torn Bangladesh in the early 70s, he overcame numerous obstacles to become a civil engineer. Today, along with his wife Karabi, Bipad hosts a Durga Puja celebration in their garage, preparing all the food with love and fostering unity in their community.

Bipad is also a loving father to his son, Arup, instilling in him the values of hard work, resilience, and giving back. Through his own dedication and selflessness, Bipad sets an inspiring example for Arup, encouraging him to make a positive impact in the world. Together, as a family, they embody the spirit of service, inspiring others to create meaningful change within their community.
