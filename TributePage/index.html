RABINDRANATH TAGORE

THE BARD OF BENGAL

Here's a time line of Rabindranath Tagore's life:

  • 1861 - Born on May 7 in Calcutta, India
  • 1878 - Tagore began writing poetry as a child
  • 1883 - Tagore published his first poetry collection under the pseudonym Bhanusimha
  • 1890 - Tagore began managing his family's estates in Shilaidaha, a region now in Bangladesh
  • 1898 - Tagore's wife and two of his children died
  • 1901 - Tagore founded an experimental school in rural West Bengal
  • 1901 - Tagore wrote the collection of poems called "Gitanjali"
  • 1913 - Tagore won the Nobel Prize in Literature
  • 1915 - Tagore was knighted by the British King George V
  • 1941 - Tagore died on August 7 in Calcutta, India
